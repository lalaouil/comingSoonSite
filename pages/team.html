The Team

Bahia Alaoui

Bahia Alaoui is a co-founder, and the CEO of Gets You Inc. She received her bachelor’s degree in psychology and her master’s degree in Counseling with an option in Marriage, Family, and Child Counseling, both from California State University Fresno. As a Marriage and Family Therapist trainee at Fresno Family Counseling Center, she provided counseling services to children, adults, families, and couples. Bahia will continue to gain experience as a Marriage and Family Therapist Intern until she becomes a licensed therapist.

In 2012, Bahia began working for Dr. Nicolson as a Behavior Therapist at the Autism Center at Fresno State. When Dr. Nicolson founded the California Autism Center, Bahia was one of the first few employees to be hired on. Bahia began her position at the California Autism Center as a Behavior Therapist, was eventually promoted to Case Assistant, and then to Clinical Supervisor.

Bahia has combined her knowledge and experience from both fields and positions to hold support groups for parents of children on the autism spectrum, as well as one for siblings of children on the spectrum. Though she is responsible and a hard worker, she is also a fun-loving free-spirit who enjoys being in nature and spending time with friends and family.

Laurie Alaoui

Laurie left college to move to Morocco in 1978 where she lived for 12 years. While there, she worked with several small charities devoted to helping the poor and the disabled in Casablanca and Marrakech. She began her family and then returned to the United States and college, where she pursued a degree in Creative Writing at San Francisco State University, culminating in a job as the restaurant editor for the San Francisco Independent and later working in the print advertising industry.

After raising her children, she became enthralled with the tech industry and its potential to make positive changes in the world, so she began learning to code at home with the help of various websites and Railsbridge. She graduated from General Assembly's WDI bootcamp in 2015 and has been coding ever since.

Laurie considers the San Francisco Bay Area home, but she is currently residing in the Sacramento area. She is Nana to three adorable grandchildren and the proud mother of three, with her youngest now serving as her CEO!

Henry Jackson

Henry has over 20 years' experience working in technology industries developing and architecting solutions. Henry was owner and Chief Systems Architect of his own firm for ten years, H.A.J Computer Consulting Group Inc., in which he worked with Northrop Grumman to develop the Advance Technology Transit Bus (A.T.T.B) system. He has worked with such companies as ESPN, Disney, Experian and many others.

Henry has worked with numerous technologies including Java, Ruby on Rails, Node.js, Reactjs, Objective C, Heroku, and he has created Single Page Applications, Web, and Mobile applications.

When Henry is not dealing with technologies, he enjoys mountain bike riding, basketball, fishing, and most of all being with his family.

Amanda Nicolson, Ph.D., BCBA-D

Amanda Nicolson received her bachelor’s degree at Oklahoma City University, her M.A. in Psychology from the University of Nevada, Reno, and her Ph.D. from Florida State University. She became a Board Certified Behavior Analyst in 2000. She was the director of in-home services for ABC School in Sacramento and consulted with other autism service agencies in California before moving to be lead behavior analyst for Washoe County School District in Reno, NV.

In 2006 Dr. Nicolson moved to Fresno, CA to accept a faculty position at Fresno State where she remained for 8 years as an associate professor in the Psychology department. She also served as the coordinator for the Master’s program in ABA and founded the autism center at Fresno State. Dr. Nicolson left the university in order to pursue full time clinical work and co-founded the California Autism Center where she remained for 3 years.

In other professional endeavors; she has published articles in peer reviewed journals and chapters in several books as well as co-authoring the recent book, Behavioral Principles in Communicative Disorders: Applications to Assessment and Treatment.  Dr. Nicolson has served as a reviewer for several journals and was a reviewer for the National Autism Center’s National Standards Project. She also regularly presents at regional and national conferences.  Dr. Nicolson was recently re-elected to the Board of Directors for the California Association for Behavior Analysis where she will serve a two year term. She has traveled to Bosnia and the country of Georgia to work on autism projects in those countries. In 2017 she opened Swan Consulting Inc. in Fresno, to continue providing comprehensive, contemporary ABA services for children and families affected by ASD in a unique model. Last (but not least), Amanda is the proud mother of two wonderful children.
